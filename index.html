Demand-Based Planning

We source only what's needed based on real-time store demand, eliminating overstock and spoilage at the retail level.

🚛

Efficient Direct Logistics

Cutting out middlemen means fewer handling points, shorter transit times, and significantly less damage to produce.

🌡️

Controlled Storage

Temperature-monitored storage at every stage preserves freshness longer, keeping vegetables viable and reducing waste.

🌱

Composting Partnerships

Even unsold organic matter is diverted to composting programs, ensuring zero-landfill waste from our store.

About Fresco

Built on a Simple
Belief

"We believe food should be fresh, transparent, and trusted. By combining traditional farm sourcing with simple technology like QR traceability, we aim to create a smarter and more honest vegetable supply chain."

Fresco was born from the frustration of not knowing where your food comes from. We set out to fix the broken agriculture retail chain — not just for consumers, but for the farmers who grow our food and deserve fair recognition and better prices.
